The following are the awards that Muhammad Ali had recieved in chronological order.

  1. 1959: National Golden Gloves Light Heavyweight Champion
  2. 1959: National Amateur Athletic Union champion
  3. 1960: National Golden Gloves Light Heavyweight Champion
  4. 1960: National Amateur Athletic Union champion
  5. 1960: Gold medal, Rome Olympics, light-heavyweight boxing
  6. 1964-67: World Heavyweight Champion
  7. 1970: Dr. Martin Luther King Memorial Award
  8. 1974: Sportsman of the Year, Sports Illustrated
  9. 1974: Fighter of the Year, Boxing Writers Association
  10. 1974-78: World Heavyweight Champion
  11. 1978-79: World Heavyweight Champion
  12. 1979: Honorary Doctorate of Humane Letters, Texas Southern Univesity
  13. 1979: Street named after him in Louisville, Kentucky
  14. 1985: Recognized for long, meritorious service, World Boxing Association
  15. 1987: Elected to Boxing Hall of Fame
  16. 1990: Inducted into International Boxing Hall of Fame
  17. 1996: Lights Olympic torch, Atlanta
  18. 1997: Arthur Ashe Award for Courage, ESPN
  19. 1997: Essence Living Legend Award
